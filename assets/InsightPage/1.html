Darjeeling

Explore the Queen of the Hills

Darjeeling, known as the "Queen of the Hills," is one of the most famous hill stations in India. Located in West Bengal, it is renowned for its stunning views of the Kanchenjunga range, tea gardens, and colonial-era charm. The town is a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Day-to-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Darjeeling and check-in at your hotel. Explore the Mall Road and take a leisurely walk through the famous Chowrasta. Visit the Darjeeling Zoo in the afternoon, followed by the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute.
  • Day 2: Early morning visit to Tiger Hill to witness the stunning sunrise over the Kanchenjunga peaks. Visit the Batasia Loop and Ghoom Monastery, followed by a relaxing afternoon tea in one of Darjeeling's famous tea gardens.
  • Day 3: Take a ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train) to enjoy the scenic beauty of the hills. Visit the Peace Pagoda and explore the local markets for some shopping.
  • Day 4: Visit the Tea Gardens and the Botanical Garden. Spend the afternoon shopping for local handicrafts and souvenirs. Head back home with unforgettable memories of your hill station retreat.

Traveler Tips

  • Carry a light jacket or sweater, as the weather can be chilly, especially in the mornings and evenings.
  • Try the local Darjeeling tea, which is world-renowned for its flavor and aroma.
  • If you want to visit Tiger Hill, be prepared for an early morning rise to catch the stunning sunrise.
  • Wear comfortable shoes, as Darjeeling has hilly terrain, and there is a lot of walking to do.
