Murshidabad

Discover the Historical Heart of Bengal

Day-to-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival at Murshidabad, visit Hazarduari Palace, a historical landmark, and Nizamat Imambara, a beautiful mosque.
  • Day 2: Explore Katra Mosque and Jafarganj Cemetery, then take a scenic boat ride on the Ganges River.
  • Day 3: Visit Moti Jheel and Khosh Bagh, a tranquil garden where you can enjoy a peaceful afternoon.
