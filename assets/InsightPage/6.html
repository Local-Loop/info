Sundarbans

Explore the Majestic Mangrove Forest

The Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the largest tidal halophytic mangrove forest in the world, home to the majestic Bengal tiger. Visitors can explore its unique ecosystem, rich biodiversity, and secluded waterways by boat.

Day-to-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival at Sundarbans, followed by a boat ride through the forest.
  • Day 2: Wildlife safari to spot tigers and other wildlife in the Sundarbans Tiger Reserve.
  • Day 3: Explore the local fishing villages and enjoy the rustic charm of the delta.
