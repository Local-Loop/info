Bishnupur

Explore the Terracotta Temples and Rich Heritage

Bishnupur, located in the Bankura district of West Bengal, is a town known for its rich history, terracotta temples, and ancient architecture. The town is famous for its classical music, particularly the Bishnupur Gharana of Indian classical dance, and its unique terracotta art and architecture.

Day-to-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Bishnupur. Explore the ancient temples like Radha Gobinda Temple and Jor Bangla Temple. In the evening, enjoy a local classical dance performance.
  • Day 2: Visit the famous Bishnupur Terracotta Temples. Explore the Bishnupur Museum and enjoy a traditional pottery workshop.
  • Day 3: Attend a local art exhibition and visit the Shyamrai Temple. Return in the evening.
