Mandarmani

Relax at the Serene Beaches of Mandarmani

Mandarmani, located on the southeastern coast of West Bengal, is one of the cleanest and most peaceful beach destinations in India. Famous for its wide sandy shore, gentle waves, and red crabs crawling on the beach, Mandarmani is ideal for a relaxing weekend escape.
