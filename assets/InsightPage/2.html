Puri

Discover the Sacred City and Its Stunning Beaches

Puri is a vibrant beach city located on the eastern coast of India in Odisha. Known for its sacred Jagannath Temple and beautiful beaches, Puri is a major pilgrimage destination as well as a popular tourist spot for beach lovers.

Day-to-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Puri. Visit the famous Jagannath Temple, followed by a relaxing evening on Puri Beach.
  • Day 2: Full day beach activities and a visit to the Konark Sun Temple and Chandrabhaga Beach.
  • Day 3: Explore local markets and the Puri Beach festival (if in season). Evening at the beach for sunset.
  • Day 4: Visit the Chilika Lake, a vast brackish water lagoon, before departing.
